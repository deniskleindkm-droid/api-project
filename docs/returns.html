Returns & Refunds

Last updated: June 2026
01

30-Day Return Window

We want you to love what you ordered. If you are not completely satisfied, you may return your item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

02

Item Condition

To be eligible for a return, items must be unworn, undamaged, and in their original condition. All original packaging, tags, and protective materials must be included. Items showing signs of wear, alteration, or damage will not be accepted.

03

How to Initiate a Return

Email us at hello@mikisi.co with your order number and the reason for your return. Our team will respond within 1–2 business days with return instructions and a shipping address.

Please do not send items back without first contacting us — returns sent without prior authorization may not be processed.

04

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.

Please allow additional time for your bank or card issuer to post the credit to your account.

05

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility. We recommend using a tracked shipping method as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit. Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

06

Contact Us

Questions about your return? We're here to help.
