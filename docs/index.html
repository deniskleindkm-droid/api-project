Care ▾

Yours for years to come.

Sterling silver is a living metal — worn often, it only gets better. A few simple things keep it beautiful:

  • Wear it often — your skin's natural oils protect the silver.
  • Keep it dry — store it dry, away from moisture.
  • Keep it clean — remove before swimming, showering, or perfume.
  • Bring back the shine — a soft cloth restores its glow in seconds.

Silver naturally deepens over time — never a flaw, always a sign it's real.
