Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
01

Introduction

Mikisi operates mikisi.co. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

02

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Name and email address (account registration)
  • Shipping address (order fulfillment)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)
Automatically Collected
  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our website and shopping experience
  • Send promotional emails (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations
04

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe (payment processing) — stripe.com/privacy
  • Shipping carriers (USPS) for order delivery
  • Service providers who assist our operations under strict confidentiality agreements
05

Social Media

We use TikTok, Pinterest, Instagram, and Facebook APIs to publish content about our products. We do not collect or store social media user data. Our social media integrations post content to our own accounts only and do not access your personal social media profiles.

06

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged into your account
  • Remember your cart items
  • Analyze website traffic (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some features of mikisi.co may not function properly without cookies.

07

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmission is encrypted via SSL/TLS. Payment processing is handled by Stripe which is PCI DSS compliant.

08

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to fulfill orders and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

09

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights contact hello@mikisi.co.

10

Children's Privacy

Mikisi does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information please contact us immediately.

11

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your California rights contact hello@mikisi.co.

13

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.
