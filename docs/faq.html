About Our Jewelry

Is Mikisi jewelry real silver? ▾
Yes. Every Mikisi piece is made from genuine 925 sterling silver — 92.5% pure silver, the real precious metal. Each piece is stamped "S925" as proof of authenticity. It's real jewelry, not plated brass or costume fashion.
Why is Mikisi more affordable than other silver brands? ▾
We keep things honest. You're paying for real 925 sterling silver and thoughtful design — not a luxury markup. We believe quality jewelry should be something everyone can wear every day, so we price fairly and let the product speak for itself.
Will it turn my skin green? ▾
No. Real 925 sterling silver doesn't turn your skin green — that's something that happens with cheap plated or brass jewelry. Mikisi pieces are hypoallergenic and gentle on sensitive skin.
Does sterling silver tarnish? ▾
All real silver naturally darkens over time — it's completely normal and not a flaw. In fact, it's a sign your piece is genuine silver. A soft polishing cloth brings back its shine in seconds.
What finishes do you offer? ▾
Depending on the piece, our jewelry comes in rhodium (silver), 18K gold, rose gold, and white gold finishes — all applied over solid 925 sterling silver.

Caring for Your Piece

How do I keep my jewelry looking its best? ▾
Sterling silver is a living metal — worn often, it only gets better. Wear it regularly (your skin's natural oils protect it), keep it dry and stored in an airtight bag or lined jewelry box when not worn, and remove it before swimming, showering, or applying perfume. If it dulls, a soft cloth restores its glow.
